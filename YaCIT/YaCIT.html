Instructions

On each turn, roll the dice up to 3 times to get the highest scoring combiniation for one of the six categories.

Click on the dice to keep them and then click on Roll.

After you finish rolling, you must select a score or a zero in one of the categories. The game will end when all categories are filled in.

The score will be added up as you select your score and a bonus of 35 points will be added if your score is greater than 62.

You may choose the color of your dice at any time during the game.

Have fun and enjoy.
