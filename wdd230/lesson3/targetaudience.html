Target Audience

The target audience is any person that is wanting to find out what the weather is so they can either decide what they want to do that day or see if the weather is right for the activity they would like to do.

Scenarios:
• A young man is wanting to go on a motorcycle ride and wants to know if it is going to rain. Pending on the weather, it will allow him to make the decision to ride or not.

• A couple is planning on going on a picnic for the evening. They want to make sure that they weather will be nice enough for a picnic or not.

• A father and son are wanting to spend some bonding time together. They want to go fishing so they check the weather to see what the weather is going to be. They can make the decision if it will be a good day to fish or not.

• A farmer needs to get his corps planted. He will be able to see if that day would be a great day to do so or not.

• A group of people are wanting to go skying. They knew it snowed the day before but they want to see if the weather will be nice enough to take on the powder snow or not.
